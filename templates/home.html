Communication CoachBot Platform

Practice difficult conversations with Conflict, RISE, DESC, PAUSE, and SEA CoachBots.

These CoachBots help you practice communication skills for conflict, feedback, psychological safety, emotional regulation, and structured conversation.

Choose a CoachBot

How to Use

  1. Use as Guest, Register, or Login.
  2. If using guest mode, enter your name.
  3. Choose one CoachBot.
  4. Select an Ollama model.
  5. Click a starter card or type your own message.
  6. For coaching or practice, share your own real situation.

Conflict CoachBot

Helps users navigate difficult conversations and conflict using interests, needs, and practical relationship repair steps.

RISE CoachBot

Rapport, Interest, Social Norms, Effective Messaging.

Helps users build psychologically safer communication through rapport, curiosity, norms, and effective messaging.

DESC CoachBot

Describe, Express, Specify, Consequences.

Helps users prepare assertive conversations using a structured script.

PAUSE CoachBot

Pay Attention, Acknowledge, Understand, Seek, Examine.

Helps users slow down, listen, regulate emotion, and respond with curiosity.

SEA CoachBot

State, Explain, Ask.

Helps users clearly state a concern, explain the reason or impact, and ask an open-ended question.
