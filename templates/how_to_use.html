How to Use the CoachBots

Choose one of the five CoachBots depending on the communication skill you want to practice. Each CoachBot works step-by-step using four pathways: Learn, Practice, Be Coached, and Create Training.

Which CoachBot Should I Choose?

Conflict CoachBot

Framework: Conflict Transformation

Use this when the situation involves repeated conflict, strained relationships, competing needs, identity concerns, power dynamics, or relationship repair.

It helps you look beneath the surface using the Iceberg Model: observable events, underlying needs, fears, values, identity concerns, and interests.

RISE CoachBot

RISE: Rapport, Interest appeal, Social conformity, Effective messaging.

Use this when you need influence, clarity, trust-building, shared understanding, or effective messaging.

It helps you communicate in a way that lowers resistance and increases the chance of shared understanding and action.

DESC CoachBot

DESC: Describe, Express, Suggest, Consequences.

Use this when you need to de-escalate, set a boundary, clarify expectations, or make a respectful request.

It helps you describe facts without judgment, express impact, suggest a constructive alternative, and clarify the positive outcome of change.

PAUSE CoachBot

PAUSE: Pay attention, Acknowledge your story, Understand evidence vs assumptions, Seek alternative explanations, Execute a values-aligned response.

Use this when emotions, assumptions, bias, or snap judgments may affect your response.

It helps you slow down, examine your assumptions, and choose a response aligned with your values.

SEA CoachBot

SEA: State, Explain, Ask.

Use this when you need to share your view clearly while staying curious and open to another person’s reasoning.

It helps you state your observation or view, explain your reasoning or intent, and ask a genuine question.

The Four Pathways

  1. Learn: Use this when you want to understand the framework, its purpose, and when to apply it.
  2. Practice: Use this for low-stakes rehearsal with fictional or practice scenarios.
  3. Be Coached: Use this when you want help applying the framework to your own real situation.
  4. Create Training: Use this when you want to create teaching materials, scenarios, or facilitation guidance for others.

How to Start

  1. Open the CoachBots page.
  2. Select a CoachBot.
  3. Use as Guest, Register, or Login.
  4. If using guest mode, enter your name.
  5. Select an Ollama model.
  6. Choose a starter card or type your own message.

Important

If you choose Be Coached, share your own real situation. The CoachBot will guide you one step at a time.

If you choose Practice, the CoachBot may use low-stakes fictional scenarios so you can rehearse safely before applying the framework to a real situation.
